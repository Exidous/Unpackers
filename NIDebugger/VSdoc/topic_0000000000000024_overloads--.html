NIDebugger.SingleStep Method

NIDebugger Class  See Also

Performs a SingleStep operation, that is to stay it resumes the process and pauses at the very next instruction. Jumps are followed, conditional jumps are evaluated, Calls are either stepped into or over depending on StepIntoCalls value.
